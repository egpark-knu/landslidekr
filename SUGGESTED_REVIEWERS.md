## Selection rationale

The manuscript combines (i) physically based shallow rainfall-triggered landslide modelling, (ii) multi-event retrospective benchmarking on a single national domain, and (iii) agent-orchestrated reproducible-research infrastructure. A balanced reviewer pool should cover all three, without overlapping the author's immediate national collaborative circle. Minimum intended coverage:

- **R1 — physically based landslide modelling / SHALSTAB-family.** Expert on steady-state and transient physical models (SHALSTAB, SINMAP, TRIGRS) across rainfall-triggered events; evaluates the model-choice framing.
- **R2 — remote-sensing-based Korean landslide inventories / NDVI-change scar methods.** Expert on Sentinel-2 scar detection, NDVI-change thresholding, or Korean NIDR-report integration; evaluates the reference-label layer.
- **R3 — reproducible-geoscience workflows / open benchmark design / agent-orchestrated pipelines.** Expert on reproducible-research infrastructure for geohazards (Snakemake/workflow-level benchmarks, LLM-agent orchestration, or FAIR open-science release); evaluates the execution-provenance and AgentTrace contribution.
